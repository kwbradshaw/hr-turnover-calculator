Why we built this

Every HR professional knows that losing an employee is expensive. But when it comes time to put a number on it — for a budget request, a board presentation, or a business case — most teams are left guessing or citing a generic statistic that doesn't reflect their actual situation.

WorkforceCost.com exists to change that. We build free, transparent HR calculators that give HR teams and business leaders a credible, customizable number they can actually stand behind.

Every formula we use is documented. Every assumption is adjustable. And we never pretend a configurable estimate is a published research finding — because your credibility matters.

The problem we're solving

Turnover costs are consistently underestimated because they're spread across multiple budget lines — recruiting, HR admin, manager time, lost output — none of which appear together in a single report. WorkforceCost.com brings them all into one place.
